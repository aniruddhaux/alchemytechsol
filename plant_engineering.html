Manufacturing

The Manufacturing industry forms the backbone of global trade and commerce, producing goods and commodities that are essential to humanity. It is no wonder then that the manufacturing industry heavily depends on Engineering and IT technology solution for research and innovation, enhanced production, testing, maintenance and sustenance.

At Alchemey Techsol we provide cutting-edge solutions for various types of manufacturing industry. From ‘Boot Loaders’ and ‘Device Driver Development’, ‘ PCB Design’, ‘Fabrication and Assembly’ to ‘Performance driven team structuring’ to ‘On-time resource availability’ our solutions assures seamless integration of technologies that eliminate waste and facilitate maximum output.

    Here are some services we provide in the Manufacturing space:

  • Board Support Package (BSP) Development
  • Firmware Development
  • System Software Development
  • System Validation and Testing
  • Micro Architecture Design and Implementation
